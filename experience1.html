During the semester break part four, I had internship experience within only two months at Bahagian Pengurusan Hartanah Jabatan Perdana Menteri . I was in Unit Teknologi Maklumat . I had so much fun there because I gained lot of knowledge from there and also built my confidence in done my tasks. During this internship, I was involved in various duties such as professional communication, creative design, technical problems, and data management. From each of these tasks, I have learned the importance of precision, commitment, and teamwork. I have learned how to handle important details, solve technical problems, and be a good team player. These encounters have been important in helping to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and application and gave the me a taste of the ever changing needs that a professional job possesses.

The internship also influenced me on the personal level apart from the administrative and technical skills. It brought about discipline, responsibility, and the realization of teamwork necessity. I will always hold dearly the good relations that I had with my colleagues, knowledge gained from my experiences, and encouragement received.

While closing the chapter, I got a lot of knowledge, insight, and lessons of life. This internship has been an important turning point in my career, as well as in my quest for personal development.
